The newly launched game AiliA, developed by IceToad Studio LLC, is captivating puzzle enthusiasts with its innovative mirror-based gameplay. Now available on Steam for $11.99, players can enjoy a 20% discount during its first two weeks, making it an excellent opportunity for both casual and hardcore gamers to dive into this unique experience.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating between the real world and a mirrored realm, solving over 100 intricate puzzles. The games mechanics allow mirrors to transform into interactive spaces, where players can manipulate their surroundings to unveil new paths and solutions. With visually stunning environments and engaging puzzles, AiliA promises to be a standout title in the puzzle genre.

Additionally, a special collaboration bundle with Moncage offers an extra 10% discount, appealing to fans of both games. As players explore the intertwining worlds of reality and reflection, they will uncover the heartfelt story of sisters Aria and Lia. Will you take on the challenge and discover the secrets hidden within the mirrors?
