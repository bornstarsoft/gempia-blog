Square Enix is making waves in the gaming industry by partnering with Google to introduce an AI chatbot in its popular MMO, Dragon Quest X. This innovative feature, powered by Google Gemini, aims to enhance the player experience by providing a conversational companion named Chatty Slimey, designed to assist new players and guide them through the game.

During a recent press event, Takashi Anzai, head of development, emphasized that this AI companion will ensure that newcomers wont feel lost or lonely as they embark on their gaming journey. Chatty Slimey will not only respond to player inquiries but will also analyze game events, initiating conversations during key moments such as defeating powerful enemies or discovering rare items.

This move reflects a broader trend in the gaming industry, where AI technologies are increasingly being integrated to improve player engagement and streamline development processes. As Square Enix aims to automate a significant portion of its QA and debugging through generative AI in the coming years, the question remains: how will these advancements shape the future of gaming?
