SEGA is gearing up for a monumental celebration as Puyo Puyo marks its 35th anniversary in 2026. Following the recent festivities for Sonic the Hedgehog, the company has unveiled the "Dairensa!" project, which promises a series of exciting initiatives to commemorate this beloved puzzle game. Fans can look forward to a special live stream featuring voice actors and live demos, alongside a host of merchandise and events across Japan.

From February pop-up stores in major cities to a collaborative lottery featuring both Sonic and Puyo Puyo, SEGA is ensuring that fans have plenty to look forward to. The anniversary celebrations not only highlight the enduring popularity of Puyo Puyo but also showcase SEGA's commitment to engaging its community through unique experiences. As we anticipate more announcements, one can't help but wonder what surprises SEGA has in store for its dedicated fanbase.
