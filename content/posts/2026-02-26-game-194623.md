Hungry Studio's flagship title, Block Blast!, has been recognized as the "Best Puzzle Game" at the Sensor Tower APAC Awards 2025, highlighting its remarkable global impact and player engagement. This accolade, determined by key metrics such as download velocity and user retention, underscores how the game has resonated with players across more than 200 countries.

The success of Block Blast! can be attributed to its innovative gameplay mechanics, which offer a unique "drag-match-disappear" experience. With its availability in over 40 markets and a strong presence in the APAC region, Hungry Studio has effectively localized its strategies, including high-score challenges in Japan and celebrity collaborations in Korea. This approach has transformed Block Blast! from a global phenomenon into a beloved local favorite.
