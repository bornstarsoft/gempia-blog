Released in 2016, "Stephen's Sausage Roll" has become a cornerstone of modern puzzle game design, influencing developers and players alike. This game, often compared to the classic "Sokoban," has spawned a new genre of puzzle games dubbed 'sausage-like,' showcasing its profound impact on the gaming landscape.

Set on a whimsical island, players navigate a character armed with a fork and a sausage, solving intricate puzzles that require strategic movement and planning. The game's unique mechanics, such as rolling sausages and cooking them on a barbecue grill, create a challenging yet rewarding experience, earning it an impressive 90 Metascore and an overwhelmingly positive reception on Steam.
