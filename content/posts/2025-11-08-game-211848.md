In a recent update, BioWare has put to rest the growing concerns surrounding the development of the next Mass Effect game. Despite rumors fueled by EA's impending sale to private investors and a reduction in BioWare's development team, executive producer Mike Gamble has confirmed that the beloved franchise is very much alive and in active development. This reassurance comes as a relief to fans who have been eagerly awaiting news since the teaser trailer was released two years ago.

Gamble emphasized that the team is fully focused on creating new stories within the Mass Effect universe, stating, "We have a lot of universe to cover, lots of features to build, and lots of romances to figure out." This commitment to the franchise is further underscored by the announcement of a live-action series set after the original trilogy, promising fresh narratives that will not simply retread Commander Shepards journey.
