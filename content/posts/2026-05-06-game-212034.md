In a significant move for the tech industry, former Naver Cloud executive Lee Dong-soo has announced the launch of his new AI startup, focusing on agent-based AI computing solutions. This initiative marks a pivotal shift as he seeks to build a team capable of developing innovative computing systems tailored for the emerging agent AI era.

Lee's vision involves creating a dual framework that understands both the characteristics of AI agents and the infrastructure constraints they operate within. The startup is currently seeking talent across four key areas: agent frameworks, machine learning inference, agent product evaluation, and agent optimization. With the first hiring phase closing on the 12th, this venture promises to contribute significantly to the AI landscape.

As the demand for advanced AI solutions continues to grow, Lee's startup could play a crucial role in shaping the future of agent-based computing. Will this new venture set a precedent for future AI innovations?
