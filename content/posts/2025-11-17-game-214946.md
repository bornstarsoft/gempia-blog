They Will Come is an exciting new entry in the world of video games, blending steampunk aesthetics with horror elements to create a unique adventure puzzle experience. Developed by Game Pop Studio, led by industry veteran Jeff Troutman, this game promises to immerse players in a richly detailed alternate history where they must navigate through a world filled with danger, including towering automatons and the ominous Wretched Ones.

Players will take on the role of Little Benjamin, armed with a trusty slingshot and electrobanger, as he embarks on a quest to rescue his captured family. The gameplay will focus on exploration, item collection, and puzzle-solving, all while maintaining a stealthy approach to avoid the lurking threats. With its captivating visuals and a bittersweet narrative, They Will Come is set to leave a lasting impression on fans of the genre.

As we look forward to its release on PC via Steam in 2026, the anticipation builds for what promises to be a thrilling blend of storytelling and gameplay. Will you be ready to face the horrors that await in this steampunk nightmare?
