Get ready for an enchanting gaming experience as ININ, in collaboration with Studio Siesta and Rocket Engine, announces the release of Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam. This charming shooter, featuring 12 adorable witches, is set to launch digitally on December 15 for Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4, with a physical special edition available on December 9. 

The special edition is a treasure trove for fans, including the physical game, an original soundtrack, a full-color artbook, and a digital download code for Cotton DLC. Trouble Witches FINAL! promises a visually stunning experience with its hand-drawn 2D designs and reimagined 3D backgrounds, alongside exhilarating bullet-hell gameplay that will challenge even the most seasoned players. With unique characters and endless replayability, this game is sure to captivate both casual gamers and hardcore fans alike.

As we look forward to the release, its exciting to see how this beloved franchise continues to evolve. Will you be joining the adventure with these magical witches?
