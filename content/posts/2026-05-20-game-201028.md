Harry Krueger, the visionary director behind the acclaimed PS5 exclusive Returnal, has embarked on an exciting new journey by founding Cosmic Division, a game studio based in Finland. After nearly 15 years at Housemarque, Krueger aims to carve out a unique identity for his new venture, drawing on his extensive experience in creating award-winning action games. This transition marks a significant moment in the gaming industry, as it highlights the ongoing evolution and innovation within game development.

Cosmic Division's first project promises to deliver an unapologetically gameplay-first experience with a narrative that resonates emotionally with players. With seed funding already secured, the studio is actively seeking collaborators and exceptional talent to help bring this vision to life. Krueger's commitment to building on timeless gaming values while exploring fresh themes is a refreshing approach in today's rapidly changing landscape.

As we look ahead, the formation of Cosmic Division raises intriguing questions about the future of gaming. Will Krueger's new studio redefine single-player experiences in a world increasingly dominated by multiplayer games? Only time will tell, but one thing is certain: the journey of cosmic wonder has just begun.
