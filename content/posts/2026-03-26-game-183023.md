Agni-Flare Co., Ltd. has just announced the pre-orders for its highly anticipated ultra-high-speed competitive puzzle game, SHIKA-Q, which will be available on the Nintendo Switch starting March 26, 2026. This game promises to redefine the puzzle genre by combining the strategic depth of traditional puzzles with the fast-paced action typical of fighting games, making it a must-try for both casual and competitive gamers.

Scheduled for a worldwide release on April 9, 2026, SHIKA-Q will not only be available on the Nintendo Switch but also on PlayStation4, PlayStation5, and Steam. With a unique gameplay mechanic that emphasizes real-time decision-making and psychological warfare, players will need to think and act faster than ever before. The games vibrant soundtrack and immersive visuals are designed to enhance the overall experience, making it as thrilling to watch as it is to play.

As we look forward to the launch, its exciting to think about how SHIKA-Q could shape the future of competitive gaming. Will it become the next big eSports phenomenon? Only time will tell!
