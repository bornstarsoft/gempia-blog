Bloober Team, known for its work on titles like Silent Hill 2 and Layers of Fear, has recently teased an intriguing Nintendo exclusive titled Project M. CEO Piotr Babieno claims that this unannounced project will resonate with fans of iconic horror franchises such as Resident Evil, Silent Hill, and Eternal Darkness. He hinted that Project M will introduce a unique gameplay experience that could only be realized on Nintendo hardware, promising a bold twist that sets it apart from existing horror games.

This announcement comes after a previous mention of Project M in a Polish interview, where Babieno expressed his commitment to collaborating with top game creators to ensure that the game exceeds expectations. As a passionate Nintendo fan, Babieno's vision for Project M reflects his long-standing dream of creating unforgettable gaming experiences for the platform. With such high expectations, fans are left eagerly anticipating the first details of this mysterious project.
