The excitement is palpable as Zachtronics announces the release of Opus Magnum: Complete Edition for Nintendo Switch, set to launch on March 17, 2026. This open-ended puzzle game, which has already garnered a strong fanbase on PC, will include the highly anticipated prequel, De Re Metallica, making it a must-have for both new players and returning fans.

In Opus Magnum, players will step into the shoes of Anataeus Vaya, the Head Alchemist of House Van Tassen, as they master the intricate machinery of alchemical engineering. With features like designing machines, solving open-ended puzzles, and engaging in a rich narrative filled with intrigue, this game promises to deliver hours of captivating gameplay. Plus, the inclusion of a solitaire minigame offers a unique way to take a break from the intense puzzle-solving action.

At a price point of $29.99, with a pre-order discount of 20%, Opus Magnum: Complete Edition is shaping up to be a fantastic addition to the Nintendo Switch library. Will you be diving into the world of alchemy when it launches?
