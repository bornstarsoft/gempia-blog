After two decades of captivating players, the beloved MMORPG Fallen Sword is finally receiving a sequel, aptly named Fallen Sword 2. Announced by Hunted Cow Games, this new installment promises to blend the nostalgia of the original with modern gameplay elements, set to launch in Q3 2026. For fans of classic turn-based RPGs, this is a thrilling development that highlights the enduring appeal of retro gaming.

Fallen Sword 2 will take players back to the enchanting world of Erildath, offering a fresh adventure while retaining the core features that made the original a hit. With turn-based combat, heroic classes, and guild battles against Titans, the sequel aims to deliver a familiar yet innovative experience. Importantly, the original Fallen Sword will continue to thrive alongside its successor, ensuring that long-time players can enjoy both worlds.

As anticipation builds, players are encouraged to wishlist Fallen Sword 2 on Steam to unlock exclusive in-game rewards. This exciting announcement not only revitalizes the franchise but also invites a new generation of gamers to experience the charm of classic RPGs.
