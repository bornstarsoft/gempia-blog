Microsoft has officially rolled out Xbox Mode for Windows 11 PCs, providing users with a controller-focused full-screen experience. This new mode allows gamers to browse and launch their favorite titles through an optimized interface, making it easier than ever to switch between Xbox Mode and the traditional Windows desktop.

The introduction of Xbox Mode marks a significant evolution from the previously limited full-screen experience available on Windows handhelds. Microsoft has taken player feedback into account, enhancing the experience across various devices, including laptops and desktops. Users can now access a comprehensive game library, including titles from Xbox Game Pass and other leading PC storefronts.

While availability is currently limited to select markets, users are encouraged to enable the Get the latest updates as soon as they are available toggle in their settings to receive the update when it reaches their region. As Microsoft continues to innovate in the gaming space, it will be interesting to see how Xbox Mode shapes the future of gaming on Windows 11.
