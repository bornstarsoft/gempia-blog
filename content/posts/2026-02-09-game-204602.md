Get ready for a refreshing take on puzzle-solving with the upcoming release of "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" on February 10, 2026. This innovative point-and-click game invites players into a dreamlike world where the key to solving puzzles lies in the creative arrangement of objects like scraps of paper and leaves. Unlike traditional puzzle games, the mechanics allow for free placement of items, encouraging players to think outside the box and discover solutions through exploration and intuition.

As the third installment in the "HER TREES" series, this game builds on the success of its predecessor, "HER TREES: THE PUZZLE HOUSE," which garnered a Very Positive rating on Steam. With over 40 unique puzzles to tackle, it promises to be a standalone experience that is accessible to newcomers while still providing depth for returning players. The games non-verbal nature and monochromatic design make it inclusive and visually striking, appealing to a wide audience.

With a launch discount of 15% and a bundle offer with the previous game, "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" is not just a game; it's an invitation to immerse yourself in a world of creativity and relaxation. Are you ready to rearrange your way to victory in this enchanting puzzle adventure?
