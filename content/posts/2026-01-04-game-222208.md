The release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console Game marks a significant milestone for fans of NASCAR gaming, particularly with its extensive selection of base paint schemes. This game not only introduces a variety of drivers but also includes paint schemes from the three NASCAR National Series and the ARCA Menards Series, making it a must-play for racing enthusiasts. 

While some popular sponsors and paint schemes are notably absent, such as those from Monster Energy and certain alcoholic beverage brands, the game still showcases fan favorites like Red Bull, which makes its return to NASCAR gaming after a long hiatus. This blend of nostalgia and new offerings creates a rich experience for players who appreciate the aesthetics of racing.
