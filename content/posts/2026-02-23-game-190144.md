Are you ready to turn your beloved pet into a video game character? Merge Donuts, the charming Suika-style puzzle game, is hosting its first-ever Furry Friends fan contest, inviting pet owners to submit photos or videos of their furry companions for a chance to immortalize them as collectible donut characters in-game. This unique opportunity not only celebrates our pets but also adds a delightful twist to the gaming experience.

Running from February 18 to March 4, the contest encourages participants to showcase their pets personalities through creative submissions. Whether you have a playful pup, a curious cat, or even a feathered friend, all pets are welcome. The winners will not only see their pets transformed into whimsical donut characters but will also receive custom digital artwork, making this a memorable experience for both pets and their owners.

So, if you've been looking for a fun way to engage with your pet and the gaming community, now is the perfect time to join in the excitement. Will your pet be the next donut superstar?
