In a bold move to reshape the gaming landscape, Arjan Brussee, co-founder of Guerilla Games and former technical director at Epic Games, is developing 'The Immense Engine'a game engine designed to provide a distinctly European alternative to the dominant American and Chinese technologies like Unreal Engine and Unity. Brussee emphasizes that this initiative is not just about gaming; it opens doors for 3D simulations in various sectors, including defense and logistics, all while adhering to European regulations.

Brussee's vision for The Immense Engine includes innovative construction principles and full AI integration, which he believes will revolutionize how game engines are developed. He critiques existing engines for their reliance on traditional user interfaces, suggesting that the rise of AI necessitates a new approach to software development. By leveraging AI, Brussee argues that developers could achieve in a fraction of the time what would typically require a larger team.

As the gaming industry continues to evolve, the introduction of a European-centric engine could not only diversify the market but also enhance the creative possibilities for developers across the continent. Will The Immense Engine set a new standard for game development in Europe? Only time will tell.
