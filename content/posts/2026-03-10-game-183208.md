If you're a fan of cozy gaming experiences, then "Kitty Haven" is a title you won't want to miss. This upcoming puzzle game invites players to create a perfect sanctuary for their feline friends, each with unique personalities and needs. From picky eaters to feisty furballs, you'll need to solve engaging puzzles to ensure peace and happiness in your kitty haven.

The game not only emphasizes strategic thinking but also allows players to express their creativity by unlocking new items and residents. With a demo available on Steam, players can dive into this charming world and experience the joy of designing their own cat sanctuary. As we look forward to its full release, one can't help but wonder: how will this game redefine the cozy gaming genre?
