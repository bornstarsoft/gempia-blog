As the gaming community eagerly anticipates the outcome of the 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards, Nintendo's Switch 2 is making waves with its impressive lineup of games. Notably, both *Mario Kart World* and *Donkey Kong Bananza* have been nominated for the coveted 'Console Game of the Year' award, showcasing the platform's strong debut in the gaming landscape.

The nominations highlight not just the quality of the games but also the potential of the Switch 2 as a leading gaming console. With *Mario Kart World* competing for 'Best Multiplayer' and *Donkey Kong Bananza* recognized for 'Best Audio Design', its clear that Nintendo is setting high standards for both gameplay and technical excellence. The competition is fierce, with titles like *Death Stranding 2* and *Ghost of Ytei* also in the running, making this year's awards particularly exciting.

As we look forward to the announcement of the winners on November 20th, one has to wonder: will Nintendo's latest innovations secure them a spot at the top? The gaming world is watching closely, and the anticipation is palpable.
