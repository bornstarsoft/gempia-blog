## What We Can Help With

Please contact us if you need help with:

- In-app purchases
- Missing hearts or rewards
- Ad-related issues
- App crashes or loading problems
- Word, pronunciation, or meaning issues
- Localization or translation issues
- General feedback about the game

When contacting us, please include as much information as possible:

- Your device model
- iOS or Android version
- App version
- A screenshot, if available
- A short description of what happened

## In-App Purchases

KPOP Word Search may offer optional in-app purchases such as heart packs or an Agency Support Pass.

If you experience a purchase issue, please first try the **Restore Purchases** option in the app settings if available.

If the issue continues, contact us with:

- The product name
- Approximate purchase time
- Your device type
- A screenshot of the issue, if available

Please do not send full payment card information or sensitive financial information.

## Fictional Content Notice

**Gempia Entertainment is a fictional in-game company.**  
It is not a real entertainment agency and does not conduct real auditions.

The app does not use real idol names, real artist images, real music, real agency branding, or real audition programs.

For more details, please see:

[Audition Notice](https://gempia.com/auditions)

Some visual and pronunciation audio assets may be pre-generated production assets.
